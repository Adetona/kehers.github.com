Packages

Sakamanje provides a range of packages that assist students as young as fourteen to begin thinking about their careers and provides them with the tools and support to aid them in achieving their career aspirations. Sakamanje also works together with Government Bodies and Private Organisations to create an educational curriculum which promotes a pro-active mindset towards career development for both teachers and students, while also creating and marketing employment opportunities for the students after they complete their education. Sakamanje considers it necessary to engage all stakeholders in this industry (students, teachers, law makers and employers) simultaneously to enable the cultural shift towards more informed career decision.

Sakamanje’s services are broken down into four categories

 

High School Students

We believe that starting early enables a seed to be planted and nurtured, shaping the students’ thoughts about education and career aspirations.

University Students

Sakamanje also aims to assist University students to build upon the theories you are taught with solid practical experience that will differentiate you when it comes to securing employment.

School Leavers

Sakamanje works with this group to advise, guide and nurture their decisions based on facts, skills, experience and market demands.

Private & Public Sector Bodies

Sakamanje aims to work with the necessary partners to introduce a new curriculum that focuses on life skills, career development and regional development.
