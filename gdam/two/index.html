EDAM Nigeria evolved from the Asset Management unit of Express Discount Ltd (EDL) following the significant growth in the asset portfolio of the company and the outstanding performance of its asset management business. To maximize investment opportunities and returns from this line of business, EDAM was birthed and incorporated in May 2005 as a private limited liability company and licensed by the Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC) and the Nigerian Stock Exchange (NSE) to engage in Asset Management Services and act as an Investment House.

EDAM Nigeria was until December 10, 2014, a wholly owned subsidiary of Express Discount Limited (EDL) now in liquidation, one of the five Discount Houses registered to carry out, transact and trade in government issued securities in Nigeria. Express Discount Limited (EDL) was recently declared insolvent by Central Bank of Nigeria (CBN) and a Liquidator, Mr. Babatunde Ajayi of Babington Ashaye & Co. was appointed by CBN.
