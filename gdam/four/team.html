Profile of Management Team

The Management team is headed by two executive directors: Abimbola Ogundokun (MD /CEO) and Oyekemi Ayeye.

Other members of the team include:

Peter Shodipo (Group Head Risk & Compliance)

Prior to joining EDAM Nigeria, Peter worked with Stanbic IBTC and Chartered Bank Plc. He has banking and investment advisory experience spanning over a period of fifteen years. He is Fellow (FCA) member of The Institute of Chartered Accountants of Nigeria (ICAN) and an Associate member of Chartered Institute of Taxation of Nigeria (CITN). He is also a certified Forensic Accountant (CFA) and a member of the Chartered Institute of Bankers of Nigeria (CIBN). He holds an MBA degree from Obafemi Awolowo University, Ile-Ife. He is also certified by the Balanced Scorecard Institute USA as a Balanced Scorecard Professional (BSP) and holds ACCA in International Financial Reporting Standards (IFRS). He is currently the president of Committee of Chief Compliance Association of Capital Market Operators in Nigeria (CCCOCIN).

Henry Ogbuaku (Group Head, Research and Funds Management)

Henry joined EDAM Nigeria from Fidelity Union Securities Limited (FUSL) (a subsidiary of Fidelity Bank Plc) where he was Head of Research. Prior to joining EDAM, he lectured at the department of Economics, University of Lagos. Henry holds a B.Sc in Economics from the University of Benin and a Masters’ Degree in Economics from the University of Ibadan. He has also attended various professional trainings and conferences in advanced financial analysis and investment management. Henry is also a student member of the Chartered Institute of Stockbrokers.

Tunde Thomas [Group Head, Credit and Treasury]

Tunde is an experienced investment banker with a bachelor’s degree in Economics and MBA both from Obafemi Awolowo University, Ile –Ife. He has over 15 years industry experience in banking and has attended various courses both locally and internationally including the prestigious Stanford Graduate Business School. He joined EDAM in March from Weiji Capital LLC, where he was the Director of Deal Originations for West Africa.

Olawale Balogun (Head, Stockbroking Division)

Olawale is actively involved in the trading of equities and fixed income instruments, providing investment solutions with a mix of financial assets to varying investors/clients’ needs ranging from risk averse to risk seekers. He has over ten years work experience in the Nigerian Capital Market. Olawale has invaluable knowledge of the trend in Nigeria Capital Market and its interaction with the global financial markets that has positively impacted in the spectrum of solutions to our clients. He has always been part of the team saddled with the responsibility of providing portfolio management and advisory services to high net-worth and institutional clients. Prior to joining Express Discount Asset Management Limited, he was a Senior Associate in BGL Securities Limited and Senior Officer in the operations unit of Nigerian Stockbrokers Limited (Subsidiary of Sterling Bank Plc.). He is a scholar of Oxford Brookes University, UK, Chartered Financial Analyst (CFA), a member of the Chartered Institute of Stockbrokers (CIS), Institute of Chartered Accountant of Nigeria (ICAN), Association of Certified Chartered Accountant (ACCA) UK, and Chartered Institute of Taxation of Nigeria (CITN). He has attended several local trainings with great relevance to investment advisory and portfolio management.

Mr. Oluyemi Adekoya [Head, Information Technology (IT)]

Oluyemi is an Information Systems Architect with tertiary degrees in Chemical Engineering, Computing and Information Systems. His working experience spans across Manufacturing, Telecoms, Oil & Gas and Finance sectors of the Nigerian Economy. He currently is the Head, Strategy and Information Systems in EDAM Nigeria. Apart from possessing several vendor specific certifications which include Microsoft & Cisco, he is also a member of ISACA (Information Security Audit and Control Association) as a Certified Information Systems Auditor (CISA). He is certified by the Balanced Scorecard Institute USA as a Balanced Scorecard Professional (BSP).

Oluwasina Olubu [Head, Research]

Oluwasina joined EDAM Nigeria from BGL Plc where he was an economic and equity research analyst. His work experience spans across research, corporate finance and advisory, asset management and structured finance which he garnered having worked in various organizations like BGL, Kedari, Meristem and Diamond bank. Oluwasina holds a First class degree in Economics from the University of Ado- Ekiti and was the overall best graduating student from the University in the year 2007. He also holds a Masters’ Degree in Economics from the University of Lagos. He has attended various professional trainings and conferences in advanced financial analysis and investment management. Sina is also an Associate of the Chartered Institute of Stockbrokers and a dealing clerk of the NSE.
